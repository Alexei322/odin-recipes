Teriaki chicken

Description

A Teriaki chicken recipe

Ingredients:

  • Sesame oil
  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spring onions
  • Sticky rice
  • Bok choi

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a non-stick pan over a medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken and fry for 7 mins, or until golden.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and fry for 2 mins.
  4. Stir in the honey, soy sauce, vinegar and 100ml water.
  5. Bring to the boil and cook for 2 - 5 mins over a medium heat until the chicken is sticky and coated in a thick sauce.
  6. Scatter over the spring onions and sesame seeds, then serve the chicken with the rice and steamed veg.
