Chilli pasta

Description

A Chilli pasta recipe

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Chilli
  • Chicken
  • Thyme

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta, bring to boil in a large pan
  3. Fry chicken
  4. After pasta is cooked, add cooked chicken
  5. Once pasta is cooked, remove water and add sauce on a low heat
  6. Add chicken
  7. Serve with basil
