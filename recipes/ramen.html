Ramen

Description

A ramen recipe

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Chicken stock
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Chilli
  • Sesame oil
  • Chicken
  • Spring onion

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles and chicken stock, bring to boil in a large pan
  3. Add chopped chilli
  4. After noodles are cooked, add cooked chicken
  5. Serve with teaspoon of sesame oil and chopped spring onion
  6. Finally, add a fried egg
